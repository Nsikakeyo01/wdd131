Nsikak’s Nigerian Flavors | Website Plan

Site Name

Nsikak’s Nigerian Flavors

I chose this name because it reflects my passion for authentic Nigerian cooking and my personal take on sharing traditional recipes with a modern twist. It’s a way to showcase the rich culinary culture of Nigeria through my perspective.

Optional domain availability: nsikaksflavors.com

Site Purpose

This website will serve as my personal hub for Nigerian recipes, from classic dishes like Jollof Rice and Egusi Soup to lesser-known regional delicacies. It will provide easy-to-follow instructions, ingredient tips, and my personal notes on cooking each dish.

Scenarios

  • How can I cook Egusi Soup in a way that’s authentic but simple for beginners like me?
  • Where can I find traditional Nigerian dessert recipes, and how do I adjust them for my small kitchen?

Color Schema

  • Main Color: #E65100 (deep orange) – used for headings, accents, and navigation highlights.
  • Background Color: #FFF8E1 (warm cream) – used as the page background for a cozy, food-friendly feel.
  • Accent Color: #D32F2F (red) – used for call-to-action buttons, links, and important highlights.

Typography

  • Georgia, serif: used for headings to give a touch of elegance and tradition.
  • Verdana, sans-serif: used for body text for readability and a modern feel.
